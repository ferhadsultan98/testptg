About Us

Our story, mission, and vision

Prime Trade Group is a leading procurement and tender management consultancy based in Azerbaijan. Founded in 2015, we've built a reputation for excellence in helping organizations optimize their procurement processes, reduce costs, and ensure compliance with local and international regulations.

Our team consists of procurement specialists, supply chain experts, and industry professionals with extensive experience across various sectors. We combine local knowledge with international best practices to deliver tailored solutions that meet our clients' specific needs.
